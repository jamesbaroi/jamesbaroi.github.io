SPC BAROI
IT SPECIALIST | U.S. ARMY

Profession of Arms

I am an active duty, 25B IT Specialist, enlisted in the United States Army, stationed at Joint Base Lewis McChord, in the 5th Battalion, 20th Infantry Regiment.

I serve the people of the United States by providing mission critical IT support to soldiers in my battalion. My responsibilites include unclassified (NIPR) and classified (SIPR) systems management, user account provisioning for the same, IT process development and automation, equipment life cycle management, and Information System Security Officer (ISSO) and Telephone Control Officer (TCO) services.

Certification

I hold the CompTIA Security+ (Sec+) certification and I am currently working towards the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification. Future goals include the CompTIA Cybersecurity Analyst (CySA+) and Penetration Testing (PenTest+) certifications.

Experience

I joined the Army in 2023 and have over two years of experience working in IT. Prior to enlistment, I worked for five years as a research associate at California State University Fresno, Department of Chemistry and for three years at the same teaching undergraduate chemistry.

Education

I received my Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science from the University of California Riverside in 2010, and my Master of Science in Chemistry from California State University Fresno in 2018.
