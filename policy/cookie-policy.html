2. Consent Methods

Users have the option to consent to the use of all cookies used on this site or to consent to select categories of cookies. Users also have the option to decline the use of all cookies on this. If users do not decline the use of all cookies on this site then users must consent to the use of all necessary cookies.

2.1 Accept Cookies

If users have not previously consented to the use of at least one category of cookies on this site, then users may consent to the use of all cookies on this site by clicking on the "Accept" button that appears along with the Cookie Policy notice displayed on any page of this site that requires the use of cookies.

2.2 Select Cookies

If users have not previously consented to the use of at least one category of cookies on this site, then users may consent to the use of select categories of cookies by clicking on the "Customize" button that appears along with the Cookie Policy notice displayed on any page of this site that requires the use of cookies. Clicking on the "Customize" button on the Cookie Policy notice toggles the display of a selection window that lists each category of cookies used on this site. This selection window may be used to select one or more categories of cookies to which the user is willing to consent. By clicking on the "Confirm" button that appears within the selection window, users may consent to the use of the selected categories of cookies.

This option for providing consent to the use of cookies on this site does not allow the user the option to deselect the category of necessary cookies thereby necessitating the user's consent to the use of necessary cookies on this site.

2.3 Decline Cookies

Users may choose to decline the use of any cookie on this site by clicking on the "Decline" button that appears along with the Cookie Policy notice displayed on any page of this site that requires the use of cookies. Clicking on the "Decline" button will remove the Cookie Policy notice from the page for as long as the page is not refreshed or reloaded.

Users may choose to decline the use of any cookie on this site also by not interacting with the Cookie Policy notice. This method will persist indefinitely the display of the Cookie Policy notice on this site.
